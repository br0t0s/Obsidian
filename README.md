## About
✨ This repository contains my personal **Obsidian templates** used to organize notes, improve workflows, and speed up writing inside Obsidian.  
These templates are designed to make note-taking cleaner, more consistent, and easier to manage across different use cases such as daily notes, projects, ideas, study sessions, and knowledge management. ✨

## How I Use Them
These templates help me create structured notes quickly without starting from scratch every time.  
They are especially useful for keeping a consistent format across my vault and improving productivity during writing and planning.

## Usage
<details>

<summary><b>⚡ How to Use in Obsidian</b></summary>
<br>

1. Copy the templates into your Obsidian vault.
2. Open **Settings** in Obsidian.
3. Enable the **Templates** core plugin.
4. Select your templates folder.
5. Insert templates into your notes whenever needed.

</details>
